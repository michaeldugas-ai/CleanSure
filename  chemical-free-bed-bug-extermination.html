Why Chemical-Free Treatment Matters

Traditional pesticide treatments, while effective, leave behind chemical residues that can pose risks to your family's health. Chemical-free heat treatment offers several critical advantages:

  • Safety first: No toxic fumes, residues, or chemical exposure
  • Immediate re-entry: Your home is safe to return to the same day
  • Complete elimination: Kills all life stages, including pesticide-resistant bugs
  • Comprehensive reach: Heat penetrates areas chemicals cannot reach
  • Environmentally responsible: No harm to indoor air quality or the environment

How Our Heat-Based Process Works

Our chemical-free heat treatment uses precise thermal control to eliminate bed bugs naturally:

🔥 The CleanSure Heat Treatment Process

1
Initial Assessment: Our technicians conduct a thorough inspection to determine the extent of infestation and develop a customized heat treatment plan.
2
Equipment Setup: Specialized heating equipment is positioned throughout affected areas, with sensors placed to monitor temperatures precisely.
3
Heat Application: Temperatures are gradually raised to 120-140°F and maintained for several hours to ensure complete elimination of all bed bug life stages.
4
Monitoring & Control: Technicians continuously monitor temperatures throughout the space, ensuring even heat distribution and proper exposure times.
5
Final Inspection: After treatment, we conduct a thorough inspection and provide follow-up support under our satisfaction guarantee.

Heat penetrates mattresses, furniture, baseboards, and even the smallest cracks where bed bugs hide. Unlike chemicals that may not reach every hiding spot, heat treatment provides comprehensive coverage throughout the treated space.

💡 Did You Know?

Bed bugs die when their body temperature reaches 113°F for just a few minutes. Our treatment maintains temperatures well above this threshold throughout your home, ensuring complete elimination even in the most protected hiding spots.
